# Badge

They're useful when you want to add additional info. Like a version number of a project or for status messages for individual items.

<Playground>
    <Badge>34 unread messages</Badge>
</Playground>

## Colours
Badges come as default, brand, info, warning, success and error

To use, add the **type** prop:

<Playground>
    <Badge>Default</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="brand">Brand</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="info">Info</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="warning">Warning</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="success">Success</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="error">Error</Badge>
</Playground>

## Rounded
Make your badges rounded with the **rounded** prop.

<Playground>
    <Badge rounded>Default</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="brand" rounded>Brand</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="info" rounded>Info</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="warning" rounded>Warning</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="success" rounded>Success</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="error" rounded>Error</Badge>
</Playground>

## Ghost
Remove your badges bodies with the **ghost** prop.

<Playground>
    <Badge rounded ghost>Default</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="brand" rounded ghost>Brand</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="info" rounded ghost>Info</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="warning" rounded ghost>Warning</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="success" rounded ghost>Success</Badge>
    <br />
    <Badge type="error" rounded ghost>Error</Badge>
</Playground>
