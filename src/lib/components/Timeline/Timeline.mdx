# Timeline
Timelines are good to illustrate a connected set of events that have happened or are planned to happen.

## Standard
<Playground>
    <Timeline>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Nothing
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Big Bang
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Galaxies go everywhere
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Solar systems form
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Earth appears
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Human infestation occurs!
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item last>
            All this has happened before, and will happen again
        </Timeline.Item>
    </Timeline>
</Playground>

## Loading last item

Use the **loading** prop on both the _Timeline_ and _Timeline.Item_ components to indicate the last item hasn't happened yet.
<Playground>
    <Timeline loading>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Nothing
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Big Bang
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Galaxies go everywhere
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Solar systems form
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Earth appears
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Human infestation occurs!
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item last>
            All this has happened before, and will happen again
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item loading>
            ...Evolution
        </Timeline.Item>
    </Timeline>
</Playground>

## Alternating items

Use the **left** prop on a _Timeline.Item_ to put it on the left side of the Timeline.

Make sure to put the **alternate** prop on the _Timeline_ as well.

<Playground>
    <Timeline loading alternate>
        <Timeline.Item>
            Nothing
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item type="brand">
            Big Bang
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item type="info" left>
            Galaxies go everywhere
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item type="warning">
            Solar systems form
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item type="success" left>
            Earth appears
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item type="error">
            Human infestation occurs!
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item last left>
            All this has happened before, and will happen again
        </Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Item loading>
            ...Evolution
        </Timeline.Item>
    </Timeline>
</Playground>
