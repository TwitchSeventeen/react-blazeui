## Ordered
The **ordered** prop will apply correct numbering to ordered lists and nested lists.

<Playground>
    <List ordered>
        <List.Item>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</List.Item>
        <List.Item>Consectetur adipiscing elit</List.Item>
        <List.Item>Nulla volutpat aliquam velit
            <List ordered>
                <List.Item>Phasellus iaculis neque</List.Item>
                <List.Item>Purus sodales ultricies
                    <List ordered>
                        <List.Item>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</List.Item>
                        <List.Item>Consectetur adipiscing elit</List.Item>
                    </List>
                </List.Item>
                <List.Item>Vestibulum laoreet porttitor sem</List.Item>
                <List.Item>Ac tristique libero volutpat at</List.Item>
            </List>
        </List.Item>
        <List.Item>Faucibus porta lacus fringilla vel</List.Item>
    </List>
</Playground>

## Un-styled
Applying the **unstyled** prop to a List will remove the bullet points.

You can also remove bullet points on individual Items by adding the **unstyled** prop.

<Playground>
    <List unstyled>
        <List.Item>This list is unstyled</List.Item>
        <List.Item>Consectetur adipiscing elit</List.Item>
        <List.Item>Nulla volutpat aliquam velit
            <List>
                <List.Item>Phasellus iaculis neque</List.Item>
                <List.Item>Purus sodales ultricies</List.Item>
                <List.Item unstyled>This is an unstyled item</List.Item>
                <List.Item>Ac tristique libero volutpat at</List.Item>
            </List>
        </List.Item>
        <List.Item>Faucibus porta lacus fringilla vel</List.Item>
    </List>
</Playground>

## Inline
To make your list items flow left to right apply the **inline** prop.

<Playground>
    <List inline>
        <List.Item>Lorem</List.Item>
        <List.Item>ipsum</List.Item>
        <List.Item>dolor</List.Item>
    </List>
</Playground>
